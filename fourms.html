Meet Our Team

We are here to help you with your mental health journey.

Katty Nelson - Licensed Therapist

As a therapist, I focus on helping clients rebuild self-esteem and navigate relationship challenges. I became a therapist because I’ve always been passionate about understanding emotions and supporting others during difficult moments. Seeing people grow into stronger, more confident versions of themselves is what motivates me every day.

Aliza Carson - Licensed Counselor

As a counselor, I work closely with individuals to help them navigate emotional challenges, stress, and personal growth. I became a counselor because I’ve always believed in the power of guidance and supportive conversation. My goal is to provide a safe space where clients feel heard, understood, and empowered to move forward.

John Smith - Licensed Therapist

I work as a licensed therapist helping individuals manage anxiety, depression, and major life transitions. I became a therapist because growing up I saw how many people struggled in silence, especially men who felt they couldn’t speak up. I wanted to be someone who listens without judgment and helps people find healthier ways to cope and heal.

Tucker Christensen - Licensed Physician

As a physician, I focus on both physical and mental well-being to help patients achieve better overall health. I chose this career because I wanted to make a direct, lasting impact on people’s lives through care and prevention. Supporting patients through diagnosis, treatment, and recovery is what drives my work every day.

Jaylin Watkins - Licensed Social Worker

As a social worker, I support individuals and families by connecting them with resources and advocating for their well-being. I became a social worker because I wanted to help people overcome social, emotional, and economic barriers. My passion is empowering communities and helping clients build more stable, healthy lives.

Alec Enriquez - Licensed Physician

I work as a physician providing comprehensive medical care while emphasizing the importance of mental health in physical wellness. I became a doctor out of a strong desire to help others and solve complex health challenges. Building trust with patients and delivering compassionate care is central to my practice.
